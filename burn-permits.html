Burn Permits

Burn permits are required for any open burning within the Covelo Fire Protection District. These permits ensure community safety and help reduce the risk of wildfires.

How to Obtain a Burn Permit

  • Visit the Covelo Fire Station at 75900 Covelo Road, Covelo, CA
  • Call the station at (707) 983-6719
  • Apply online through CAL FIRE: burnpermit.fire.ca.gov

Burning Guidelines

  • Only burn on designated burn days
  • Clear at least 10 feet of space around your burn area
  • Keep water and a shovel on hand at all times
  • Never leave a fire unattended
  • Do not burn plastics, tires, or other prohibited materials

For more information on safe burning practices, please contact Covelo Fire Department or visit the CAL FIRE website.
